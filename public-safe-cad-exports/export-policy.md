# Export Policy

Status: `scaffolded`

## Rule

Do not publish or route CAD screenshots, STEP/STL files, renders, generated outputs, or USD handoff notes unless the source is synthetic or explicitly reviewed public material.

## Forbidden Export Sources

- production F3D files;
- production CAD;
- exact product dimensions;
- manufacturing packages;
- product geometry;
- customer parts;
- private rack models;
- internal company product names;
- sealed geometry;
- production thermal posture;
- customer-sensitive design context.

## Review Record

Every export candidate needs a completed `templates/export-review-template.md` entry before public use.
