# Public Boundary

Status: `scaffolded`  
Purpose: public/private/sealed boundary for CAD and mechanical design studies

## Public-Safe

This repository may contain:

- synthetic mechanical design studies;
- qualitative constraint tables;
- public export policy;
- CAD redaction rules;
- serviceability notes;
- conceptual airflow and ducting notes;
- non-production manufacturing assumptions;
- Mermaid diagrams;
- review templates.

## Private / Sealed

This repository must not contain:

- production F3D files;
- production CAD;
- exact product dimensions;
- manufacturing package;
- product geometry;
- customer parts;
- private rack models;
- internal company product names;
- sealed geometry;
- production thermal posture;
- customer-sensitive design context.

## Artifact Rule

CAD screenshots, STEP/STL exports, diagrams, generated outputs, and render references require explicit public-safe review before use.
